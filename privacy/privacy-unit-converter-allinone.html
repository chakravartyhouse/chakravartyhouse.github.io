Privacy Policy

Unit Converter Pro - All in One

This Privacy Policy explains how Unit Converter Pro - All in One handles information when you use the app.

The app is not yet live on the Microsoft Store. This privacy page is published in advance to support future release and transparency.
Developer Payel Chakravarty
Last updated April 18, 2026

Overview

Unit Converter Pro - All in One is designed as a utility app for unit conversions and related calculation workflows. The app is intended to be simple, useful, and respectful of user privacy.

Information collected

The app is designed to work without requiring account creation or personal profile setup.

  • No name, email address, phone number, or payment information is required to use the core features.
  • No conversion values that you enter are intended to be collected as personal data.
  • No personal documents, contacts, photos, or messages are accessed by the app for normal operation.

How information is used

If limited technical data is ever processed for app stability, security, or error diagnosis, it will only be used to maintain and improve the app experience. The app is not intended to use personal information for advertising or unrelated profiling.

Offline use

Core conversion features are intended to be available without requiring you to submit personal information. Where possible, the app experience is designed to remain useful even without continuous online interaction.

Third-party services

If future versions of the app use Microsoft Store infrastructure, crash reporting, analytics, or similar platform services, those services may process limited technical information under their own terms and privacy policies. This page may be updated when such integrations are introduced.

Data sharing

Personal information is not intended to be sold to third parties. Any data processing, if introduced in future builds, would be limited to operating, securing, or improving the app, or to meeting legal requirements.

Children's privacy

The app is not specifically directed toward children under the age required by applicable law to provide consent on their own. If you believe personal information from a child has been provided inappropriately, please contact the developer to request review or deletion.

Policy changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or store-related updates. The latest version will always be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about Unit Converter Pro - All in One, use the contact details or official links published on the Chakravarty House website.

By using the app after release, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy as applicable to the then-current version of Unit Converter Pro - All in One.
