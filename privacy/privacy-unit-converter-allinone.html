# Privacy Policy — Unit Converter Pro - All in One **Last updated: April 2026** **Developer: Payel Chakravarty** **Contact: spiceland21@gmail.com** ## Data We Collect Unit Converter Pro does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data (conversion history, settings, preferences) is stored **locally on your device only** in an encrypted SQLite database at: `%LOCALAPPDATA%\UnitConverterPro\app.db` This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. ## Crash Logs The app writes a local crash log at: `%LOCALAPPDATA%\UnitConverterPro\crash.log` This file stays on your device and is never transmitted. ## Ads (Future Update) A future version of this app may display advertisements served by Microsoft pubCenter. Microsoft's privacy policy for advertising applies: https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement You can opt out of interest-based ads via: Windows Settings → Privacy & Security → General → "Let apps show me personalized ads" ## In-App Purchases (Future Update) Purchases are processed entirely by Microsoft Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. ## Children This app is rated Everyone (3+) and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. ## Contact For questions: spiceland21@gmail.com
