Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Crush Calculator

Introduction

Thank you for using Crush Calculator. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled when you use our app.

This privacy policy is applicable to the Crush Calculator app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Payel Chakravarty (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service. This service is provided "AS IS".

Respect for Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. The Crush Calculator app is designed for entertainment purposes only and does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly.

No Personal Data Collection

The app does not require you to register, log in, or submit any form of identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or location. All calculations and interactions take place locally on your device.

What This Means

  • No Registration Required: You can start using the app immediately without creating an account
  • Local Processing: All FLAMES calculations happen on your device, not on our servers
  • No Data Storage: Names entered for calculations are not saved or transmitted
  • Anonymous Usage: We cannot identify individual users or track their behavior

Use of Third-Party Advertising (Appodeal)

This app uses Appodeal Ads SDK to show advertisements. While we do not collect any personal data, Appodeal may collect limited, non-personal data automatically to serve personalized or contextual ads.

Data That May Be Collected by Appodeal

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID (can be reset by user)
  • Approximate Location: Based on IP address (not GPS)
  • App Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage patterns
  • Crash Reports: Technical error reports

For more details, please refer to Appodeal's Privacy Policy.

No Data Storage or Sharing

Crush Calculator does not:

  • Store any user data on servers
  • Share any data with third parties (aside from what is required by Appodeal to display ads)
  • Request access to sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location (unless explicitly used for Appodeal geotargeting)

Entertainment-Only Disclaimer

Crush Calculator is a light-hearted entertainment app. It provides playful results based on name inputs and does not represent real relationship compatibility. All results are to be taken humorously and are not scientifically validated.

Important Notes

  • Results are generated using the FLAMES algorithm for entertainment purposes
  • These calculations have no scientific basis or real-world accuracy
  • Do not make important life decisions based on app results
  • The app is intended for fun and social entertainment only

Your Rights and Ad Preferences

Although we do not collect personal data ourselves, you may still control how advertising data is used through your device settings:

Controlling Ad Personalization

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads and opt-out of personalized ads
  • Advertising ID Reset: You can also reset your Advertising ID regularly for added privacy

If you wish to learn more about the data Appodeal collects or exercise your rights (e.g., access, deletion), please contact Appodeal directly via their website linked above.

Location Information

This Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

No GPS location data is collected or used by the Crush Calculator app.

Offline Functionality

The Crush Calculator app works completely offline. All FLAMES calculations are performed locally on your device without requiring an internet connection.

Benefits of Offline Mode

  • Privacy: No data is transmitted to external servers during calculations
  • Accessibility: Works anywhere, anytime without internet dependency
  • Speed: Instant results without network delays

Zero Data Collection by App

The Crush Calculator app itself does not collect any personally identifiable information from users. The only data collection occurs through the third-party advertising SDK (Appodeal) as described above.

What We Don't Collect

  • Names entered for calculations - processed locally only
  • Calculation results - not stored or transmitted
  • User behavior patterns - not tracked by the app itself
  • Personal identifiers - not collected by the app

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for teens and family use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Age Appropriateness

  • Recommended Age: 13+ years
  • Family-Friendly: Safe for all age groups
  • No Personal Data Collection: Especially important for younger users
  • Parental Guidance: Recommended for users under 16

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app. We encourage you to review the policy periodically for any updates.

Notification of Changes

  • In-App Updates: Policy changes will be noted in the app
  • Website Updates: This page will always have the latest version
  • Effective Date: Changes take effect from the updated effective date
