How to Use

Get started with professional navigation in 6 simple steps.

1

Install & Grant Permissions

Download from Google Play and grant location permissions when prompted for accurate navigation data.

2

Calibrate Your Device

Follow on-screen instructions to calibrate compass for accurate readings in your area.

3

Read Compass Heading

Hold your device flat and parallel to the ground for the most accurate compass readings.

4

Check Altitude

View your current elevation above sea level in both meters and feet for precise positioning.

5

Monitor Weather

Check current weather conditions and air pressure to plan your outdoor activities.

6

Plan with Sunrise/Sunset

Use astronomical data to plan your outdoor adventures with optimal lighting conditions.

Technical Details

Requirements and limitations for optimal performance.

Requirements

  • Location Permission: Required for compass heading and GPS altitude
  • GPS Access: Needed for accurate position and elevation data
  • Compass Calibration: Device must have magnetic sensor
  • Internet Connection: Required for weather data and some features
  • Modern Android Device: Android 5.0+ recommended for best performance

Limitations

  • Location Permission: Must be granted for core functionality
  • GPS Required: Altitude and some features need GPS signal
  • Calibration Dependent: Compass accuracy depends on proper calibration
  • Environmental Interference: Metal objects and magnetic fields may affect compass
  • Some Features Pro Only: Advanced features may require premium version
